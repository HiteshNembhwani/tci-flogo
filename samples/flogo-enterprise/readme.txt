All These Samples use the REST Service hosted at - https://my-json-server.typicode.com/tibcosoftware/bookstore/Book

If Flogo Enterprise is running on your localhost -

To Get all Books - You will need to hit the url - http://localhost:9999/books/ 
To Get Book By ISBN - you will need to hit the url - http://localhost:9999/books/1451648537

Note that the ISBN number will be found here - https://my-json-server.typicode.com/tibcosoftware/bookstore/Book
